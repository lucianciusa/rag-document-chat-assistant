Engine Profile: Mercedes-AMG 4.0L V8 Biturbo (M177/M178)
The handcrafted AMG M177 and M178 engines are the backbone of modern Mercedes-AMG performance vehicles, including the C63 AMG, E63 AMG, and AMG GT.

Specifications:
- Configuration: 90-degree V8
- Features: "Hot inside V" turbocharger placement (turbos sit between the cylinder banks for unmatched throttle response). 
- Power Output: Typically ranges from 469 hp to over 630 hp depending on the vehicle and tune.

Common Maintenance Notes:
- Spark Plugs: Due to extreme heat in the "Hot V" configuration, spark plugs must be replaced strictly every 30,000 miles. Misfires are common if delayed.
- Motor Mounts: The massive torque output significantly degrades hydraulic motor mounts. Replacing them around 50,000 miles is generally required for smooth idle.
- Oil Separator: Often builds negative pressure and fails. If whistling is heard at idle, the oil separator is the prime suspect.
