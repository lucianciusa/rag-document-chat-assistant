Volkswagen/Audi Dual-Clutch Transmission (DSG/S-Tronic) Maintenance Guide

The DSG (Direct-Shift Gearbox) provides lightning-fast gear changes used in the VW Golf GTI, Golf R, Audi S3, and Audi A3. While highly durable, it strictly demands precise fluid maintenance.

Service Schedule:
- The DSG transmission fluid and internal filter MUST be replaced every 40,000 miles. Failure to do so leads to mechatronic unit failure, a highly expensive repair.

Symptoms of Bad or Old Fluid:
1. "Clunking" or aggressive jerking when shifting from 1st to 2nd gear in traffic.
2. Delayed engagement when shifting from Park to Drive.
3. Slipping clutches under heavy acceleration.

Best Practices:
Unlike traditional torque-converter automatics, a DSG behaves like an automated manual. Creeping forward in traffic by riding the brake pedal causes the clutches to slip and overheat. Always leave a gap in traffic and accelerate cleanly to fully engage first gear.
