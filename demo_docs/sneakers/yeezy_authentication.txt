Authenticating the Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 involves checking several key areas:
1. The Boost Sole: Authentic "Boost" material feels plush and has a bubbly, pillowy texture with distinctive stamp marks randomly placed. Fake Boost is often stiff and uniform.
2. The Box Label: Ensure the UPC matches the inner size tag perfectly. Fake boxes often have incorrect fonts or misalignment in the gender sizing icons.
3. Center Stitching: The stitching running down the middle of the shoe should have an alternating "X" and "Square" pattern that is tightly woven.
4. Primeknit Pattern: Depending on the colorway (e.g., Zebra, Beluga), the primeknit should lack uniformity—fakes often look "too perfect" or pixelated.
